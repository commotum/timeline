Source: bf1db8-2025.pdf

## Core reasons
- Introduces BBEH as a new benchmark for evaluating LLM reasoning with increased difficulty.
- The main contribution is the creation of a dataset/benchmark covering a wide array of reasoning skills, plus evaluation of models on it.
