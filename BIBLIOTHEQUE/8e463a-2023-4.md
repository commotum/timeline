# ALCUNA: Large Language Models Meet New Knowledge (2023)
Source: 8e463a-2023.pdf

## Core reasons
- Introduces the KnowGen process for constructing artificial entities and uses it to define the ALCUNA benchmark, whose goal is evaluating LLMs on new knowledge understanding, differentiation, and association.
- Grounds its evaluation around ALCUNA, an artificial biological entity knowledge dataset specifically crafted to measure how models respond when confronted with knowledge that did not appear during their training.
