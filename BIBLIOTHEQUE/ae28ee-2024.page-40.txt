             Issue
             Imports within TYPE CHECKINGcaninduce“cyclic-import”
             Suppose you have two modules: a.py and b.py. ‘a‘ imports ‘b‘, but ‘b‘ needs ‘a‘ in order to do
             type annotations, then this is usually done by importing ‘a‘ within a ‘TYPE CHECKING‘ block.
             Unfortunately, this causes pylint to report ‘cyclic-import‘.
             Possibly related to https://github.com/PyCQA/pylint/issues/3285
