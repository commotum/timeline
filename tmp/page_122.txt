be specialized to machines, which are systems that have a medium,
so that inputs and outputs can be defined for them such that every
machine effects a function from its inputs to its outputs (actually
from the history of its inputs to its outputs). Computational systems
are machines that are capable of producing multiple functions; they
work by using some of their inputs to specify the functions that hold
between other inputs and outputs. Symbol systems are computa-
tional systems with symbols, that is, with devices for distal access.
