The important questions about architecture concern what func-
tions are to be provided by the fixed structure. An architecture
provides a boundary that separates structure from content. Behav-
ior is determined by variable content being processed according to
the fixed processing structure, which is the architecture. The fixed
structure represents a communality for a wide array of processing.
Thus, the natural question is, What is embodied in the architecture
and what is moved out into the content?
